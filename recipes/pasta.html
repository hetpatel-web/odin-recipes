Vegetarian Pasta Primavera

Description:

A light and flavorful vegetarian pasta primavera loaded with a variety of fresh vegetables, tossed in a garlic-infused olive oil, and served over your favorite pasta.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (spaghetti or your choice)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp dried Italian herbs
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook Pasta:
    • Boil pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté Vegetables:
    • Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
    • Add minced garlic, sauté until fragrant.
    • Add zucchini, yellow squash, cherry tomatoes, broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot.
    • Sauté until vegetables are tender yet still vibrant.
    • Season with salt, pepper, and dried Italian herbs.
  3. Combine Pasta and Vegetables:
    • Add the cooked pasta to the pan with sautéed vegetables.
    • Toss everything together until well combined.
  4. Serve:
    • Divide onto plates, top with grated Parmesan cheese, and garnish with fresh basil if desired.
    • Enjoy your delicious and colorful vegetarian pasta primavera!
