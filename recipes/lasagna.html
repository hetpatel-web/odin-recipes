Lasagna Recipe

Description:

A tasty and easy-to-make vegetarian lasagna featuring layers of colorful veggies, creamy ricotta, and savory marinara sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), diced
  • 1 small eggplant, cubed
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Prepare Vegetables:
    • Heat olive oil in a skillet, sauté onion and garlic.
    • Add zucchini, yellow squash, bell pepper, eggplant.
    • Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil. Set aside.
  2. Make Ricotta Mixture:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, Parmesan, chopped basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Assemble Lasagna:
    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread thin layer of marinara sauce in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    • Layer noodles, half of ricotta mixture, half of veggies, and a third of mozzarella.
    • Repeat layers, finishing with noodles, marinara, and remaining mozzarella.
  4. Bake:
    • Cover with foil, bake for 25 mins.
    • Remove foil, bake additional 15-20 mins until golden.
  5. Serve:
    • Let it rest for 10 mins, garnish with basil, slice, and enjoy!
