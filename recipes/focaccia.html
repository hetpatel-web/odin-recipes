Homemade Rosemary Focaccia

Description:

Enjoy the irresistible aroma and taste of homemade rosemary focaccia – a soft and flavorful Italian flatbread topped with fragrant rosemary and a sprinkle of sea salt.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110°F/43°C)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup olive oil, divided
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Sea salt, for sprinkling

Steps:

  1. Activate Yeast:
    • In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Mix Dough:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, 1/4 cup olive oil, and salt.
    • Add the activated yeast mixture and mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead and Rise:
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat Oven:
    • Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  5. Shape and Focaccia:
    • Press the dough into a greased baking pan, spreading it out evenly.
    • Using your fingers, make dimples in the dough.
    • Drizzle the remaining olive oil over the top, and sprinkle with chopped rosemary and sea salt.
  6. Bake:
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Cool and Serve:
    • Allow the focaccia to cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve warm and enjoy!
