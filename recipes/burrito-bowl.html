Veggie Burrito Bowl

Description:

A delicious and customizable veggie burrito bowl filled with flavorful rice, black beans, fresh vegetables, and topped with a zesty cilantro-lime dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown rice, cooked
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels, cooked
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps:

  1. Prepare Rice:
    • Cook brown rice according to package instructions.
  2. Assemble Burrito Bowl:
    • In serving bowls, layer cooked rice, black beans, corn, cherry tomatoes, avocado, red onion, shredded lettuce, and chopped cilantro.
  3. Season and Toss:
    • Squeeze lime wedges over the bowl for added freshness.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Gently toss all ingredients together.
  4. Cilantro-Lime Dressing:
    • For an extra kick, drizzle with a cilantro-lime dressing or your favorite salsa.
  5. Serve:
    • Enjoy this wholesome veggie burrito bowl as is or customize with your favorite toppings!
